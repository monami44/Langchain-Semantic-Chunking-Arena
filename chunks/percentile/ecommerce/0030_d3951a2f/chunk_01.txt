Building on the resourcebased view RBV perspective of the firm and firms competitive capabilities, this study examines the digital export drivers as means for firms to exploit opportunities brought about by digital technologies in their B2C digital marketing activities. By leveraging a unique dataset covering 102 Italybased firms of different sizes small, medium, and large, active in three different sectors design and furniture, fashion, and food and beverage, this work examines the role of firm resources to support internationalization via digital channels.
