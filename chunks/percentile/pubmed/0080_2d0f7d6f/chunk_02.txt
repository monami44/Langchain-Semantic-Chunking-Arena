They are also contrasted with enduring antiheroes to elevate roles of virtue and benevolence in longterm efforts to correct societal ills. What motivates enduring heroism is a central question. A hypothesis put forth is that distinct dimensions of eudaimonic wellbeing especially, purpose in life, autonomy, environmental mastery, and personal growth may be key elements that fuel efforts of enduring heroes. A final section underscores the need for such individuals in our era, drawing on wideranging evidence that inequality and injustice are worsening and have been exacerbated by major historical events Great Recession, COVID19 pandemic.
