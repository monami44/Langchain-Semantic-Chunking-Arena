The future of the International Health RegulationsThe COVID19 pandemic has changed many aspects of our lives, but it has not yet compelled substantial changes to the legal landscape of global health. Several proposals to revise the International Health Regulations IHR were put forward for discussion at the 75th World Health Assembly WHA, May 2228, 2022. The most ambitious revisions , proposed by the USA, included rapid sharing of pathogen genetic sequence data and introducing shorter deadlines for reporting and responding to emerging threats.
