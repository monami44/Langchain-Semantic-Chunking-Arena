The first attempts of the European Commission and Parliament to invoke Art. 71 of the Treaty on European Union against the Polish and Hungarian governments demonstrate the EUs political willingness to claim its own authority in defending core European values Art. 2 TEU in case of state disobedience. However, despite these attempts to integrate the Rule of Law concept into the overall EUs supervisory machinery, the Commissions and the Parliaments submissions indicate a lack of coherency in implementing the principle as a relevant tool to address multiple challenges arising within the EU Member States legal systems.
