NonSwiss scholars were then invited to react and to elaborate on the insights provided by the Swiss contributors. The first question addresses conceptual and methodological issues. While most authors agree that Europeanization is a process and not an outcome variable Exadaktylos and Radaelli 2012a, they disagree on how to define Europeanization and on how to study the impact of this process at the domestic level. In this Debate, and in line with the bulk of Swiss literature, we focus primarily on the Europeanization of policymaking and related outputs.
