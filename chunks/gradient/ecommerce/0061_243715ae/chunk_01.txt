Since the turn of the century especially small, local retailers struggle with the advent of ecommerce. It is hence no surprise that the retail sector is one of the hardesthit sectors in the current pandemic. To understand the short and longerterm impacts of COVID19 on retail, we conducted two surveys to understand ongoing changes in the sector. By contrasting changing consumer behavior with the actions taken by local retailers, we assess whether the current pandemic is potentially a catalyst for ecommerce in Belgium.
