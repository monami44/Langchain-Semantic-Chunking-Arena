Plain Language Summary No one listens to us, we know this, so we participated Qualitative evidence from menstruation research during the COVID19 pandemicDespite the success of the COVID19 vaccine rollout, trust in science was put to the test as tens of thousands of people who currently menstruate or people who used to menstruate e.g., postmenopausal reported experiencing changes to their menstrual bleeding or new bleeding. News stories and medical professionals denied or discounted their experiences. In response, we created a survey to assess menstrual side effects after COVID19 vaccination. The survey had an unprecedented response.
